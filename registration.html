Registration

How to register for the workshop?

The idea is to hold an informal workshop in which we can freely discuss and share ideas about ocean ecology
from both theory and data perspectives.
The workshop itself will consist of a series of short presentations, followed by extended chaired discussions to encourage a comprehensive dialogue.

Invitations have been sent via email on January 23th, 2024.

We would require confirmation of your attendance by January 31st, 2024 to better organize the workshop schedule.
Registration can be done by confirming your attendence via email to Emanuele Pigani.
Notification of attendence will be confirmed by email.

We provide to cover the costs of hosting the workshop, housing, breakfast, and lunches while travel and dinner are at your expense.

For any questions, please refer to Emanuele Pigani.
